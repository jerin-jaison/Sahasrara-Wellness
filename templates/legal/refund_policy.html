Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

No Refund Policy

Please note that all booking deposits and full payments made to Sahasrara Wellness are strictly non-refundable. This policy is essential for us to maintain our staffing and premium service standards.

1. Cancellation

If you wish to cancel your appointment, please notify us as soon as possible. While we do not offer refunds, giving us early notice helps us manage our therapists' schedules.

2. Re-scheduling

We understand that plans can change. You may re-schedule your appointment under the following conditions:

  • Re-scheduling must be requested at least 4 hours prior to your scheduled appointment time.
  • A booking can be re-scheduled a maximum of two times.
  • Subject to therapist and slot availability.

3. Booking Fees & Charges

Some premium slots or specific services may incur a 10% service charge or booking fee that is used to secure the therapist's time. This fee is non-refundable and will be clearly marked during the checkout process.

4. No-Show Policy

Failure to arrive for your appointment without prior notice will result in the forfeiture of your booking amount in full.

5. Contact for Support

For any assistance regarding your booking or to request a re-schedule, please WhatsApp us at +91 97470 00210.
