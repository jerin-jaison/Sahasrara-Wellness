Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

At Sahasrara Wellness, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly when you:

  • Book an appointment online.
  • Contact us via WhatsApp or phone.
  • Visit our branches.

This information may include your name, phone number, email address, and booking history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your bookings.
  • Send appointment reminders and updates.
  • Improve our services and guest experience.
  • Comply with legal and security requirements.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal data is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Razorpay for payment processing. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers. All payment data is handled by Razorpay in compliance with their privacy and security standards.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. If you wish to do so, please contact us at our provided contact details.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at +91 97470 00210.
